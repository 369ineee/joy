Humanities and Social Sciences

Strand Discription

The HUMMS (Humanities and Social Sciences) strand offers a comprehensive exploration of human society, culture, and behavior. Students delve into subjects such as history, sociology, psychology, economics, and political science. Through critical analysis, research, and discussion, they gain insights into complex societal issues and cultural perspectives. The program encourages students to develop empathy, communication skills, and a broader understanding of the world around them. Graduates are prepared for a wide range of careers in fields such as education, social work, public policy, journalism, and research, as well as for further studies in humanities, social sciences, or related disciplines.
