Electrical Installation and Maintenance

Strand Discription

The IEM (Electrical Installation and Maintenance) strand offers specialized education in electrical systems and maintenance. Students learn about electrical circuits, wiring installations, power distribution, and electrical safety practices. hrough hands-on training and practical exercises, they develop skills in electrical troubleshooting, repair, and maintenance of residential, commercial, and industrial electrical systems. The program also covers topics such as electrical codes and regulations, instrumentation, and renewable energy technologies. Graduates are prepared for careers as electricians, electrical technicians, maintenance technicians, or electrical engineers in the construction, manufacturing, or energy sectors.

Possible College paths

• Electrical Engineering
• Electrical Technology
• Electrical Maintenance
• Computer Engineering
• Information Technology
