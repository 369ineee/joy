Automotive Servicing

Strand Discription

The Automotive Servicing strand offers hands-on training and specialized education for students passionate about automotive technology and mechanics. Through a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical skills, students gain expertise in vehicle maintenance, repair, and diagnostics. Our program covers a wide range of topics, including engine systems, electrical systems, braking systems, and automotive electronics. With access to state-of-the-art facilities and industry-standard equipment, students receive comprehensive training that prepares them for careers in the automotive industry. Whether pursuing roles as automotive technicians, service advisors, or shop managers, graduates of the Automotive Servicing strand are equipped with the skills and certification necessary to succeed in this fast-paced and evolving field.

Possible College paths

• Automotive Engineering
• Mechanical Engineering
• Automotive Technology
• Automotive Management
• Industrial Design
• Automotive Marketing and Sales
